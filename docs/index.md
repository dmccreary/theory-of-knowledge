Welcome to the **Theory of Knowledge (TOK)** intelligent textbook — a free, open-source, interactive resource for students exploring the nature of knowledge itself.

This course is a core component of the International Baccalaureate Diploma Programme. It examines how knowledge is produced, validated, and communicated across disciplines and cultures. Through interactive MicroSimulations, structured inquiry, and interdisciplinary connections, students develop critical thinking, epistemic humility, and the ability to navigate complex knowledge systems.

## What Makes This Textbook Different?

- **Interactive MicroSimulations** that let you explore abstract epistemological concepts hands-on
- **A learning graph** with 275 interconnected concepts to guide your study
- **Free and open source** — no paywalls, no access codes
- **Aligned with the IB Diploma Programme** Theory of Knowledge curriculum
