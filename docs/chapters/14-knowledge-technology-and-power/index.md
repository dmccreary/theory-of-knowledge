# Knowledge, Technology, and Power

## Summary

Examines how digital technologies shape knowledge production (algorithms, AI, machine learning, big data, simulations) and how power structures determine whose knowledge counts — exploring epistemic injustice, gatekeeping, marginalized knowledge, and the ethics of artificial intelligence.
